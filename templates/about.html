About

Auto Exam Question Extractor

This application automates the extraction and categorization of exam questions from uploaded documents. It uses AI and NLP techniques to simplify digital exam creation for teachers, students, and institutions.

  • Purpose: Digitize paper-based tests and automate question extraction.
  • Technologies: Python, HTML, CSS, NLP, AI
  • Developer: Jay

Sample Upload Format

  1. What is the capital of France?
  (a) Berlin
  (b) Paris
  (c) Rome
  (d) Madrid
  Answer: Paris

  2. Water boils at 100°C. True or False
  Answer: True

  3. The chemical symbol for water is _____
  Answer: H2O

  4. Explain the process of photosynthesis.
  Answer: Photosynthesis is the process by which green plants convert sunlight into energy.
